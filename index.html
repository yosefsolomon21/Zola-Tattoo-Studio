Our Studio/የእኛ ስቱዲዮ

Founded in 2010, Zola Tattoo Studio has grown from a small tattoo shop to one of the most respected studios in the country. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, artistry, and customer service Our studio is designed to be a welcoming space where clients can feel comfortable during their tattoo journey. We use only premium equipment and the highest quality inks to ensure your tattoo heals beautifully and lasts a lifetime.
